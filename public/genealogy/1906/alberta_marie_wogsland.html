Alberta Marie Wogsland

1906 - 1977

Alberta Marie Wogsland was born February 16th, 1906 to Florence and Andrew Wogsland. She married Alphonse Leick and together they had five children. Later she may have used the surname Gast. Alberta died April 21st, 1977.
