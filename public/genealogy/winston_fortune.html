Winston Fortune*

c. 1813 - ????

Winston Fortune was born to Jesse and Sally Fortune around 1813. In 1834 he married Elizabeth Gowin or Goin. What became of her is unknown, but in the mid-1850s he married Sarah Mary Jane Banks, who bore him 8 children. It is also unknown when Winston expired.
