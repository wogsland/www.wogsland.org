Henrietta Haberer Wilkie

1884 - 1967

Henrietta Agnes Haberer was born February 20, 1884 to Mary and Daniel Haberer. She married Robert R. Brown July 23rd, 1902 and together they had two children. Her second child, Dorothy, died as a baby. In 1911 Henrietta and Robert divorced. That same year she married George Wilkie. Shortly thereafter, eight year old Robert moved to grandma Haberer's house. Henrietta and George had two children together. Henrietta died in 1967.
