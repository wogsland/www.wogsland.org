Hossein Vossough was born in Iran. He had two wives, Aghdaz and Fatemah, and four children. Fatemah, his second wife, lives in Alpharetta, GA, while Aghdaz lived with Hossein in Mashad, Iran. His grandchildren call him bubba joon, which means "father dear" in Farsi. On October 6, 2005 Aghdaz died in Iran.
