Torbjorn Halvorson

1682 - 1753

Torbjorn Halvorson was born in Norway in 1682. He was owner of 1/2 the Vraalstad gaard. He married Helge Einertsdotter and fathered five children. Tobjorn was apparently also a church trustee. He died in 1753.
