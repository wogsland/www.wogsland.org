Jewelie Ellen Randall

1969 - ?


Jewelie with her distant cousin Brad Wogsland in 2006
Jewelie Ellen Randall was born February 27, 1969. She met Andrew Seubert in 1988/9. Together they had two girls, Kaliska and Athena, for whom they made up the last name Sweetwater. Jewelie and Andrew were married in June of 1999 with their girls serving as flower girls. She started io herbal alchemy, named for one of Jupiter's moons. In the summer of 2005 she discovered Wogsland.org and began contributing to the site.
