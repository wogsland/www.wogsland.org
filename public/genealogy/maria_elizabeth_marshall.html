Maria Elizabeth Marshall

19?? - 2014

Maria Elizabeth Marshall, or "Betty" as she is called, was born in Maryland to Frank and Catherine Marshall. Unfortunatly, she was injured at an early age and has spent most of her life in a mental hospital. She died there February 22nd, 2014.
