Electromagnetism > Magnetic Monopoles

Electric monopoles are well established in Nature. The electron, for example, is an electric monopole. The simplest magnetic object which seems to exist, however, it the familiar magnetic dipole with its north and south poles. Dirac especially liked the idea of magnetic monopoles existing.
