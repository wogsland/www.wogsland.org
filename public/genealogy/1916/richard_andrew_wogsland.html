Richard Andrew Wogsland

1916 - 2003

Richard Andrew Wogsland was born January 27th, 1916 to Florence and Andrew Wogsland. He married Evelyn Jenner and together they adopted one child named Daniel, who was born March 3rd, 1956. Richard died April 18th, 2003.
