Raymond Augustus Greenfield

1929 - ?

Raymond Augustus Greenfield was born April 21, 1929. He was stationed with the Army in Panama when he met Sarah Pimentál. They married, and their first son, Raymond August Greenfield, was born in Panama City, Panama. They went on to have four more children and moved around quite a bit, as military families often due. Ray and Sarah live in retirement in Arizona.
