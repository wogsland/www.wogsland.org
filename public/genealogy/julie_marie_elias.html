Julie Marie Elias

1985 - ?

Kristen & Julie dressed for the weather in 2001
 Julie Elias was born in Calfornia in 1985 to Bob and Dell Elias. Her younger sister Kristen was born two years later. When she was a young girl the family moved into the Sierra Nevada Mountains in Nevada. Julie is attending college at the University of Colorado in Boulder. Currently she is in London on a study abroad program.
