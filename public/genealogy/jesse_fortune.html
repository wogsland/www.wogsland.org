Jesse Fortune

c. 1790 - ????

Jesse Fortune was born around 1790. He served in the Virginia Militia during the war of 1812. He married Sally Thomas or Thompson in 1814, although they had a son Winston circa 1813. Whether there were other children is not known, nor is much else about Jesse.
