James Travers Thompson

1871 - 1942

James Travers Thompson was born September 2nd, 1871 on Kent Island in Maryland. He married Minnie Jones and together they had at least one child: a son named Albert. James died April 25th, 1942 and was buried in Stevensville, MD.
