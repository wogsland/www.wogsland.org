Robert Jay Fortune

1956 - ?

Jay Fortune was born in Baltimore, MD on September 24, 1956. He grew up Bel Air, MD. Jay married Leslie Berry on August 16th, 1980. He graduated from Stetson University with a BBA in 1981 and works for BEERS Construction. They made a home in Marietta, GA and there had three children. In the mid-nineties Jay was transfered and the family relocated to Tampa, FL.
