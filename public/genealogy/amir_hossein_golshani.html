Amir Hossein Golshani

1983 - ?


Young Sara & Amir
Amir Hossein Golshani was born the 3rd of March in 1983 to Parviz and Joyce Golshani. He had a younger sister, Sara. Amir learned Farsi, the native language of his father, as a child. (Golshan is the town in Iran where the family is from) Amir's parents divorced when Amir was a boy and his father remarried. Amir and his sister lived with their father in Georgia and attended Chattahoochee High School. After graduating, he attended Georgia Perimeter College for a year and a half. Then Amir enlisted with the Marines and found himself assigned to warplanning (specifically as a MAGTF Enlisted Planner). He is also an accomplished musician, with some nice demos.
During Operation Iraqi Freedom Amir was stationed in Kuwait. Through his sister he became aquainted with Jaime Wogsland, whom he talked to for many hours over the internet. After Amir returned to the US in July 2003, they started dating and a year later Jaime moved to Norfolk to be closer to him. Amir and Jaime were soon wed on May 11th, 2005 in Norfolk, VA. In the summer of 2005 he was transfered to the west coast and they made a new home together in San Diego. In the fall of 2006 Amir was promoted to Seargent. In 2009 Amir & Jaime moved back to their hometown in Georgia where their daughter Alexandra was born later that year.
