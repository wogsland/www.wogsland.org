Angela Joy Gutho

1976 - 2000

Angela Joy Gutho was the second of three girls born to Mark and Hannah Gutho. She was born on the 21st of March, 1978 in Stevens Point, WI. Angela graduated from Iola-Scandinavia High School in 1996 after playing on the varsity volleyball and basketball teams for three years. Thereafter she worked as a project analyst for Intelligent Networks Inc. in Madison, WI. Tragically, Angela died in a motorcycle accident on July 14, 2000.
