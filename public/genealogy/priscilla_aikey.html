Priscilla Aikey Wogsland

c.1902 - 1998

Priscilla Aikey married Walter Wogsland in 1933. They had no children. Walter and Priscilla made a home in Burlington, VT after his early retirement. Priscilla became his fulltime caretaker after he suffered a series of strokes in 1979. Priscilla died in 1998 and Walter followed her a couple years thereafter.
