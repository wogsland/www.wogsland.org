Olav Larsson

1726 - 1800

Olav Larsson was born in 1726 on the Vaagsland gaard in Tordal, Norway. He was a farmer. In 1763 he married Marie Jonsdotter Lensegrav, with whom he had 7 children. Olav received the Oppistua part of Vaagsland gaard from his father in 1759. His wife Marie died in 1787. Olav died himself in 1800.
