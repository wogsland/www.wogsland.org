Oscar Wogsland

1893 - 1959

Oscar Wogsland, firstborn son of of to Ole and Oletta Wogsland, was born December 24th, 1893. He worked on his uncle Andrew's farm in 1914. He married Catherine Ahlers, and together they had six children. Their sixth child, Sylvia, died as an infant. Oscar lost Catherine in 1943, and died himself in 1959.
