Sara Faye Golshani

1985 - ?

Sara Faye Golshani was born January 28th, 1983 to Parviz and Joyce Golshani. Sara learned Farsi, the native language of her father, as a child. (Golshan is the town in Iran where the family is from). Sara's parents unfortunately divorced when Sara was young. Her father remarried and they lived with him in Georgia.
