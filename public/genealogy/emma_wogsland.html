Emma Wogsland Siplon

1904 - 1986

Emma Wogsland was born February 24th, 1904 to Ole and Oletta Wogsland in Hazelhurst, Wisconsin. She married Joseph Jason Siplon with whom she had two children. Emma lost him in her later years. She died May 29, 1986 in Highland Park, Illinois.
