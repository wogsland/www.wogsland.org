Robert Louis Brown

1903 - 1972


Robert's baby picture
 Robert Louis Brown was born to Robert & Henrietta Brown on November 24th, 1903.
His parents divorced in 1911 and his mother remarried George Wilkie. 8-year-old Robert soon went to live with his grandmother (Haberer).
Robert as a boy.

Robert in his Naval uniform.
 As a young man he entered the Navy Medical Corp. After his discharge, he worked painting houses with his stepfather and then as the Assistant Pro at Bellevue Country Club.
He met Christine Burns and they were married September 20, 1924. That year they also built a home in Syracuse, NY, where they lived for the rest of their lives. Together they had four children: Dick, Ginny, Bea, and Bobbie. He worked as the plant manager for the General Baking Company. Robert died March 20, 1972 of a heart attack.
