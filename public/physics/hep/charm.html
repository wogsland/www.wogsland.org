A Charmed Life

Charm is one of the six quark flavors, and Charmonion is the name given to a charm quark and it's antiparticle in a bound state. The spectrum of Charmonium bound states has long provided a probe of meson structure and the strong force.

Potential models usually use a linear plus Coulomb form along with another term:

V(r) = -k/r + ar + other term
