Gina Brumm

1895 - 1969

Gina Wogsland was born March 30, 1895 near Tomahawk, Wis. As a young woman she moved to Lansing, Michigan where she met George Brumm. They were married in 1917 and moved to Traverse City in 1929. Together they had three children. Gina lost George in 1949. She died in 1969.
