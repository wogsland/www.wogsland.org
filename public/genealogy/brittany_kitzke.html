Brittany Kitzke

19?? -

Brittany Kitzke was the son of Gloria and Keith Kitzke. She grew up in Knoxville, TN where she attended Bearden High School, graduating in 2002. She thereafter attended the Rochester Institute of Technology, graduating in 2007 in Computer Science having also studied German. Having completed schooling she pursued a career in the defense industry.
