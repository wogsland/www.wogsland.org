Olav Nottolfson

1757 - ????

Olav Nottolfson was born in 1757 to Nottolf Larsson and Kari Torbjornsdotter in Tordal, Norway. He was a farmer. In 1775 he wed Kari Olsdatter Aase, and together they had 3 boys.
