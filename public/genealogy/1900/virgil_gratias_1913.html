Virgil Gratias

1921 - 1996

Virgil Gratias was born on June 23rd, 1913 in Wisconsin. He married Mary Ellen Glenn c. 1942 and together they had a daughter, Carmen. Both also had children from previous marriages. He lost Mary Ellen in 1996 and died himself 13 December 2003.
