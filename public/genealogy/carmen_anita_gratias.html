Carmen Gleason

1946 - 1981

Carmen Anita Gratias was born to Mary Ellen and Virgil Gratias on January 12, 1946 in Spooner, Wisconsin at home in a tarpaper shack. She married Benny Gilbert Lewis Andrew Alvin Randall c. 1965 and they had 2 children. Carmen died of cancer at St Helens Hospital in Chehalis, Washington on May 22, 1981. She was buried in the Napavine cemetery near many relatives.
