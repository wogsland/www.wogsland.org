Oscar Amundson

1894 - 1976

Oscar Amundson was born March 24th, 1894 in Iola, WI. He served in World War I. He married Helma Amundson, who had previously been married to his older brother Simon, on November 21, 1944 in Iola, WI. She died in 1973. Oscar died 5 November 1976 and is buried in Zion Lutheran Cemetery, Iola.
