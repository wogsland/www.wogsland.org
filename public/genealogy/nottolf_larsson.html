Nottolf Larsson

1722 - 1807

Nottolf Larsson was born in 1722 on the Vaagsland gaard in Tordal, Norway. He was a farmer. He married Dordi Anundsdotter Lauvsted, but they had no children (most likely she died). Later he married Kari Torbjornsdotter Vraalstad, with whom he had 8 children. Nottolf received the Nordistua part of Vaagsland gaard from his father in 1759. His wife Kari died in 1792. Nottolf died himself in 1807.
