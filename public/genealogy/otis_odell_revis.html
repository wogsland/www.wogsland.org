Otis Odell Revis

1909 - 1969

Otis Odell Revis was born March 29th, 1909. He married a women named Florence, and together they had at least three children. Later he married a woman named Minnie. Otis built his own house and lived in Townsend, MD. He died April 4th, 1969.
