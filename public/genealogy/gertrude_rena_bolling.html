Gertrude Rena Bolling

1896 - 1969

A youthful portrait of Gertrude
 Gertrude Rena Bolling was born on the 3rd of February, 1896 in Baltimore, MD. Her parents were Jacob and Minnie Bolling.
She married Albert Thompson and together they had two children. Her grandchildren knew her as Nanny. She died March 20th, 1969 in Severna Park, MD and was buried in Easton, MD.
