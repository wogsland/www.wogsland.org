Mary Ellen Glenn

1921 - 1996

Mary Ellen Glenn was born to Elra and Mabel Glenn in 1921. She married Virgil Gratias c. 1942 and together they had a daughter, Carmen. Both also had children from previous marriages. Mary Ellen died in 1996.
