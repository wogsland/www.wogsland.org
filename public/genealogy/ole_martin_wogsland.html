Ole Martin Wogsland

c.1862 - 19??

Ole Martin Wogsland, the first son of of to Gunhild and Ole Wogsland, was born on the Wogsland farm near Iola, WI (probably in 1862). He often went by his middle name, Martin. Martin married Norwegian immigrant Oletta Christina Solum, widow of a fellow named Iverson. Together Martin and Oletta had nine children. They were also founding members of the Prarie Rapids Lutheran Church northwest of Tomahawk, WI in 1908. Martin died some time during or just after the First World War.
