Richard Andrew Wogsland

1916 - 2003

Richard Andrew Wogsland was born January 27th, 1916. There are conflicting sources as to whether he was the son Florence and Andrew Wogsland or they adopted him. He married Evelyn Jenner and together they adopted one child named Daniel, who was born March 3rd, 1956. Richard died April 18th, 2003.
