Lucy Margaret Downey

1867 - 1920

Lucy Margaret Downey was born on the 20th of September in 1867. In 1886 she married Richard Henry Fortune. 1901 was a year of tradegies for Lucy. In February her young daughter Mary died and in October or November her husband died in a train accident. She herself died of Pneumonia on the 18th of February in 1920.
