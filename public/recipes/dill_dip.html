Dill Dip
From the kitchen of Mary Dell Wogsland

This recipe is a favorite among Mary Dell's great granddaughters, especially with cucumbers.

Ingredients:

1 pint sour cream
½ pint mayonnaise
1 tablespoon grated onion
4 tablespoons parsley
3 teaspoons dill weed
3 teaspoons McCormick Salad Supreme Seasoning

Mix all ingredients together. Chill 6-8 hours. Serve with fresh veggies.
