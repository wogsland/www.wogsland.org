 Sylvia Ann Wogsland was born 17 November, 1931 in Nelsonville, WI. She was the third child and only daughter of Obert and Alice Wogsland.
She married James Brown and together they three Children: Rebecca, Jeffrey, and Lori.
Sylvia with her children Becky, Jeff, and Lori in 1958.

 Sylvia and Jim still live in Fort Atkinson, WI.
