Barbara Wogsland

19?? - ?

Barbara Carol Willadsen, or Barb, as those who know her call her, married Lyle Roger Wogsland in Crown Point, Indiana. He was in the United States Air Force, so they lived for a time in Japan. Together they had three children. After Lyle retired from the air force in 1968 they moved back to Wisconsin. Barb lost Lyle to cancer in 2004.
